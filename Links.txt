Options to turn on / off CEC from command
~$ echo "on 0" | cec-client -d 1 -s #turn TV on
log level set to 1
opening a connection to the CEC adapter...


~$ echo "standby 0" | cec-client -d 1 -s #turn TV off
log level set to 1
opening a connection to the CEC adapter...
